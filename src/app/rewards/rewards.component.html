Stand A Chance To Win !

The Tshwane Varsity Hackathon is hacking competition that is organized by the Tshwane University of Technology's faculty of Information and Communication Technology and Research & Innovation office.

On the day of the Hackathon, participants will be requested to come up with solutions to certain real life problems by using Technology. Each team will be allocated a mentor/coach to work with during the duration of the Hackathon.

What do you need?

You will need your laptop/desktop and internet to connect to the online event.

Who Can Participate?

The Tshwane Varsity Hackathon is open to students from TUT,UP,SMU And UNISA

Application Fee?

Joining the Tshwane Varsity Hackathon is Completely FREE !

How will teams be formed?

The teams will be formed during the workshop day
